Below is a glossary of sorts for bets available at most casino craps table. Craps is a verbal game, you call out your bets; it is good form according to some dealers to call out bets that you can set on the table yourself so they know these bets are being made by you and not your elbow/rail neighbor. If you want payout information check out the Table of Bets page.

Horn Bets: Aces(2), Ace-Deuce(3), Yo(11), Boxcars(12)

These are one roll bets that win if one number is rolled and these numbers have the fewest combinations of being made with two fair die. These bets are located in the "Horn" bet section of the table and are generally lucky winners and often losers. You wager on that bet, someone rolls, you win money or lose money on that roll.

Any Craps(2,3,12), C&E,(2,3,11,12) Horn-High(2,3,11,12), High-Low(2,12), High-Low-Yo(2,11)

These are one roll bets that have a few combinations of winning instead of just one number. The "Horn" is four bets so the money you provided to the dealers is divided by four and placed on the number, if you say "high" after "Horn" as in "Horn"+high + Aces, Ace-Deuce, Yo, or Twelve this means you are asking the dealer to divide your bet by four and any remainder from the division place it on the number you specified. If you say "High-Low" or "High-Low-Yo(11)" you are making only two "Horn" bets. Any craps and C&E are bets that win if any of the numbers occur. You wager on that bet, someone rolls, you win money or lose money on that roll.

Any Seven(7)

These are one roll bets that win if the number with the greatest number of combinations that can be with two fair die. Location various on tables, it can be above the "Horn" bets, near the "Craps" and "Yo-Eleven" bet. You wager on that bet, someone rolls, you win money or lose money on that roll.

Hop Bets\Hop Hard Bets

These are single roll bets that win if a specific combination of dice are rolled and the bet options are only for the range of numbers 4 to 10. The hop bets are specific so you would say "4-1 Hop for two bucks" or "Hop the Sixes". They will divide your wager by two if you hop a number like "Sixes or Eights" and divide it by three if you hop the seven. "Hop" bets have a "Hop-Hard" choice for the even numbers so you can say "Hop the Hard Eight" or "Hop the Hard Ten". You wager on that bet, someone rolls, you win money or lose money on that roll.

Pass Line\Come

These can be multi-roll bets that win/lose immediately or stay on the board for some time until a win or loss. These are bets that win immediately with 7 or 11 and lose immediately with 2 or 3 or 12. You wager on this bet, and if it does not win or lose immediately then the roll was between a 4 or 10 and now you want to see that number [4,5,6,8,9,10] again before a 7 to win. Wagers can not be removed once wagered.

Don't Pass Line\Don't Come

These can be multi-roll bets that win/lose immediately or stay on the board for some time until a win or loss. These are bets that win immediately with 2 or 3 and lose immediately with 7 or 11. You wager on this bet, and if it does not win or lose immediately then the roll was between a 4 or 10 and now you want to see a 7 before that number [4,5,6,8,9,10] to win. Wagers can not be removed.

Hardways Bets(4,6,8,10)

These are multi-roll bets that win on the chosen number and lose in two ways: if a 7 if rolled or if the soft-way number is rolled. Hardways are rolls where both die are the same number e.g [4,4],[5,5]. Hardways can only be made with even numbers. You wager on this bet, and if that number is made by a combination of die that are equal you win, and you lose if that combination equals that number or 7; otherwise you bet just stands until win or loss. Wagers can be removed anytime before win or loss.

Lay Bets aka Don't Buy Bets\Don't Place (4,5,6,8,9,10)

These are multi-roll bets that win if a 7 is rolled before the number [4,5,6,8,9,10] in which you chose to bet against. You wager on this bet, and if that number is rolled you lose else if a 7 is rolled you win; otherwise your bet stands until win or loss. Wagers can be removed anytime before win or loss.

Buy Bets/Place Bets(4,5,6,8,9,10)

These are multi-roll bets that win if a number [4,5,6,8,9,10] is rolled before the 7. You wager on this bet, and if that number is rolled you win else if a 7 is rolled you lose; otherwise your bet stands until win or loss. Wagers can be removed anytime before win or loss.

Big Six\Big Eight Bets(6,8)

These are multi-roll bets that win if the number is rolled before the 7. You wager on this bet, and if that number is rolled you win else if a 7 is rolled you lose; otherwise your bet stands until win or loss. Wagers can be removed anytime before win or loss.

Contract Bets

These are multi-roll bets that win can not be removed from the table once they are made and a roll has established their permanence. They include "Pass Line" and "Come" bet, their corresponding "Odds" bets you can remove.

Non-Contract Bets

These are multi-roll bets that can be picked up after a roll has established them. They include "Don't Come" and "Don't Pass" and all the corresponding "Odds" bets. "Lay", "Don't Place", "Buy", and "Place" are also non-contract bets but they have no corresponding "Odds" bet.

Single Roll Bets

These are bets that win or lose based on the upcoming roll. They include the "Field", "Any Craps", "C&E", "Horn", "Any Seven".

Odds Bets

These are bets that are not available until a multi-roll has been established. The sequence requires a "Contract" or "Non-Contract" to be made (first), a roll ot occur establishing that bet (second), now the bettor can place additional money, before a win or loss, on that wager calling it the "Odds on...+ whatever bet that has been established" (third). These odds now win or loss with the initial corresponding bet.
